Minecraft

I have been playing Minecraft in general for over 8 years now and I've been through a lot. It started with me playing Minecraft PE - 1.7.10. I was hooked. From the on I spent almost every single day getting familiar with and/or enjoying this block game. I remember when 1.12.2 - The World of Color Update - came out which added so many features and probably is one of the most iconic "additions" to Minecraft. I had been playing Minecraft PE for about 3 years then I played Minecraft Java Edition at one of my friends house, which, of course, made me want to get Minecraft on my computer. Now I play 1.8.9 PvP with the rank title Sumo Grandmaster II. I have come a long way from playing Minecraft PE on my iPad. Minecraft has cultured millions of people, and created one of the biggest internet groups. Thanks Minecraft for making my childhood the best childhood any kid could ever have.
